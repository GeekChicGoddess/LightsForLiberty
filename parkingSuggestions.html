PARKING

Parking is always one of the concerns for downtown events. Especially if you don't get downtown much. We are listing a few of the options near the park. If cost is a concern, try to get a few friends to carpool and share the cost. If you don't like driving downtown at all, you can always take an Uber or Lyft; splitting the ride with friends makes it even better.

Once parked, if you feel a little turned around, use the Google Maps App on your phone for walking directions from where you are standing to the park address.

Lot prices are based on best information available online. Prices can change if there is a special event.
